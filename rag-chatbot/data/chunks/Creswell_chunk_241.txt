QUALITATIVE, QUANTITATIVE, AND MIXED METHODS INTRODUCTIONS A general review of all introductions shows that they follow a similar pattern: The authors announce a problem and they justify why it needs to be studied. The type of problem presented in an introduction will vary depending on the approach (see Chapter 1).
