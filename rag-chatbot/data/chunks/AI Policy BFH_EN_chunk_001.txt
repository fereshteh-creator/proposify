Relying on simple prompts (system inputs) typically leads to low-quality outcomes. Enhanced results can be attained through prompt engineering techniques. • Scrutinize the AI tools’ system responses, especially when it comes to literature references, as these tools are prone to hallucination, i producing erroneous outputs.
