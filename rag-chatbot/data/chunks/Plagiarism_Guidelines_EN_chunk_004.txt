Major case Art. 6 1 A repeat offence (e.g. another act of plagiarism in the same or in a different module) or large-scale plagiarism are considered to be grave dishonest conduct as per Article 23 KNR. 2 Repeat offences and grave dishonest conduct both result in disciplinary proceedings and can lead to expulsion. 3 Should a repeat offence or grave dishonest conduct be discovered after a title has been bestowed, the title may be revoked. Coming into effect Art. 7 This regulation comes into effect on 1 January 2009.
