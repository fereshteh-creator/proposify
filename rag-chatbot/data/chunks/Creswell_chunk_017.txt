OUTLINE OF CHAPTERS This book is divided into two parts. Part I consists of steps that researchers need to consider before they develop their proposals or plans for research. Part II discusses the various sections of a proposal. Part I: Preliminary Considerations This part of the book discusses preparing for the design of a scholarly study.
