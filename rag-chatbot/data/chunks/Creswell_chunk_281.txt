A script, as used in this book, contains the major words and ideas of a statement and provides space for the researcher to insert information. The purpose of this ___________ (strategy of inquiry, such as ethnography, case study, or other type) study is (was? will be?) to ___________ (understand? describe? develop? discover?) the ___________ (central phenomenon being studied) for ___________ (the participants, such as the individual, groups, organization) at ___________ (research site). At this stage in the research, the ___________ (central phenomenon being studied) will be generally defined as ___________ (provide a general definition). The following examples may not illustrate perfectly all the elements of this script, but they represent adequate models to study and emulate.
