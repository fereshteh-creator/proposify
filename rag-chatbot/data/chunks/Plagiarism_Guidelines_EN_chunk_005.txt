von 3 Appendix to the guidelines regarding the handling of plagiarism at the Bern University of Applied Sciences Legal foundations 1.1 Law regarding the Bern University of Applied Sciences (FaG) Art. 61a Disciplinary procedure 2 Students who gravely or repeatedly violate the disciplinary code or the principle of academic integrity may be expelled from the Bern University of Applied Sciences. 1.2 Statute of the Bern University of Applied Sciences (FaSt) Art. 34 Students 1 During the course of their studies, students shall comply with the legal system in general as well as with the rules of the university. The orders of the rector, the university executive office, the heads of department and the lecturers shall be observed. 2 Students shall live up to the principle of academic integrity. A violation of the principle of academic integrity occurs when false information is given, the intellectual property of others is damaged, or research activities are compromised by other dishonest means. 3 The rector, the university executive office, the department heads and the lecturers are authorised to take necessary action against offending students in order to uphold academic standards.
