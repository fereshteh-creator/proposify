Case studies are a qualitative strategy in which the researcher explores in depth a program, event, activity, process, or one or more individuals. The case(s) are bounded by time and activity, and researchers collect detailed information using a variety of data collection procedures over a sustained period of time. Central question in qualitative research is a broad question posed by the researcher that asks for an exploration of the central phenomenon or concept in a study.
