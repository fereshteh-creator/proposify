Formal requirements • Length approx. 8 pages text • Font: Arial 11, line spacing: 1.5 Preparing the proposal You can find the proposal template on Moodle. Please fill in all the required information and use the template to prepare your proposal, then create a final PDF. The questions included in the structure are only meant as guidance and can be deleted when you finalize your proposal. For successful submission, email your proposal to both supervisors and upload it as a PDF file on Moodle.
