It should be helpful to illustrate an approach to designing and writing an introduction to a research study that researchers might use regardless of their approach. The deficiencies model of an introduction is a general template for writing a good introduction. It is a popular approach used in the social sciences, and once its structure is elucidated, the reader will find it appearing repeatedly in many published research studies.
