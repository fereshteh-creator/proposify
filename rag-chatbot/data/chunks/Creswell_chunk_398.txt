Belmont, CA: Wadsworth. Earl Babbie provides a thorough, detailed text about all aspects of survey design. He reviews the types of designs, the logic of sampling, and examples of designs. He also discusses the conceptualization of a survey instrument and its scales. He then provides useful ideas about administering a questionnaire and processing the results.
